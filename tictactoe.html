How to Play

  • Players take turns placing X or O on the grid.
  • First to make 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  • First to make 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) only in 4x4 or 5x5 wins.
  • If the board fills with no winner, it is a draw.
